Add or make changes to an apprentice

To add apprentices to your organisation’s apprenticeship service account you’ll need:

  • the apprentice’s name and date of birth
  • the start and end date of the apprenticeship
  • the total training and assessment cost
  • your training provider’s UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN)

If you need to add multiple apprentices you can upload their details together.

Change an apprentice's details

You can change an apprentice’s details if they take a break or stop learning. This will temporarily or permanently stop payments to their training provider.

Your training provider must also record any changes in their ILR submission.

Choose a training provider

Each apprentice needs an approved training provider to deliver their training.

You can give your training provider permission to add apprentices to your organisation and set up payments. You’ll need to sign an agreement and approve any information they give to us.
