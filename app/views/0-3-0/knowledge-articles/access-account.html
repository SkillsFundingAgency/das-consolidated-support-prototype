Access your account

You cannot access your apprenticeship service account if your company does not have any apprentices. We can create another account so that if an apprentice leaves, you can still access it.

Contact us to add team members to your account.

You'll need:

  • your account ID number
  • the full PAYE scheme for the account and the last YTD value
  • the name and email address of any previous apprentices that had an account
  • the name and email address of each apprentice

We are available 8am to 10pm, 7 days a week.

Reset your password

You can reset your password if you forget it.

We will send a reset code to the email address on your apprenticeship service account.
