Using your Government Gateway details

Organisations with an annual payroll over £3 million must add PAYE schemes using their Government Gateway sign in details.

You must have authority to add PAYE schemes to your organisation’s apprenticeship service account.

You’ll need to sign in using your organisation’s Government Gateway user ID and password, then ‘grant authority’ for us to use your tax information.

Government Gateway details

Your user ID can be up to 12 characters.

If you lose or forget your user ID you can contact HM Revenue and Customs. You can reset your Government Gateway password using your user ID and email address.
