Connect your organisations

Organisations are connected if they are owned by the same person, or if one organisation owns the other. Contact HM Revenue and Customs to check if your organisations are connected.

You must add each connected organisation that will contract with your training providers, then sign an agreement to allow them to spend funds.

Do not add a training provider as a connected organisation.
