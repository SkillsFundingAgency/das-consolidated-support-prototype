Reserve and approve funding

You can use apprenticeship funding to pay for the entire cost of training an apprentice (up to the funding band maximum) or just part of it.

Check if you need to pay towards the cost of training your apprentice./p>

You must reserve funding before you can add a new apprentice to your apprenticeship service account. We will then pay the training provider directly.

The earliest you can reserve funding is 6 months before the apprenticeship training start date. The apprentice can start their apprenticeship training anytime in the month you choose, or the following 2 months.

What you'll need

You can only reserve funding for one apprentice at a time. You’ll need the apprentice’s start date and training course details.

Making changes

You can make changes to the apprenticeship training without reserving new funding.

If you need to change the apprenticeship start date by more than 3 months, you’ll need to reserve new funding.
