Register for an account if you use an external payroll agent or bureau

Organisation’s that use an external agent or bureau to run their payroll can register for an apprenticeship service account.

You’ll need your organisation’s Government Gateway account sign in details. If your organisation does not have a Government Gateway account, we’ll ask you to create one.

You must not use your payroll agent’s sign in details.

Registering for an account will not affect your payroll agent or bureau’s access.
