Register for an account if you use an external payroll agent or bureau

Organisation’s can still register for an apprenticeship service account if they use an external agent or bureau to run their payroll.

You’ll need the sign in details for your organisation’s Government Gateway account. You can create an account if your organisation does not have one.

Do not use your payroll agent’s sign in details. Registering for an account will not affect what your payroll agent or bureau can access for your organisation.
