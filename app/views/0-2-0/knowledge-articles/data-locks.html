Data locks for employers

Data lock 01

You and your training provider used a different UK provider reference number (UKPRN).

What happens next

Check you have the correct UKPRN for your training provider. Either you or your training provider could have used the wrong UKPRN.

If your training provider gave the wrong UKPRN, they need to upload their ILR data for this apprentice again with the correct UKPRN.

If you gave the wrong UKPRN, you need to stop the commitment back to the start date. You should stop the commitment back to 1 August if it started in a previous academic year.

You'll need to contact us so we can add the correct start date, then you can start a new commitment with the correct UKPRN.

Contact us if you and your training provider used the correct UKPRN.

Data lock 02

You and your training provider used a different unique learner number (ULN).

What happens next

Check if your training provider has approved the commitment. It has not been approved if it’s in the ‘your cohorts’ section of your apprenticeship service account.

If your training provider has:

  • not approved the commitment, they must change the ULN before they approve it
  • approved the commitment, you must stop the commitment and start a new one with the correct ULN
