Data locks for employers

Data lock 01

You and your training provider used a different UK provider reference number (UKPRN).

What happens next

Check you have the correct UKPRN for your training provider. Either you or your training provider could have used the wrong UKPRN.

If your training provider gave the wrong UKPRN, they need to upload their ILR data for this apprentice again with the correct UKPRN.

If you gave the wrong UKPRN, you need to stop the commitment back to the start date. You should stop the commitment back to 1 August if it started in a previous academic year.

You'll need to contact us so we can add the correct start date, then you can start a new commitment with the correct UKPRN.

Contact us if you and your training provider used the correct UKPRN.

Data lock 02

You and your training provider used a different unique learner number (ULN).

What happens next

Check if your training provider has approved the commitment. It has not been approved if it’s in the ‘your cohorts’ section of your apprenticeship service account.

If your training provider has:

  • not approved the commitment, they must change the ULN before they approve it
  • approved the commitment, you must stop the commitment and start a new one with the correct ULN

Contact us if you and your training provider used the correct ULN.

Data lock 03

You and your training provider used a different standard code.

What happens next

Check you have the correct standard code with your training provider.

If your training provider has:

  • not approved the commitment, you can change the standard code on the commitment
  • approved the commitment, you need to stop the commitment and start a new one with the correct standard code

Contact us if you and your training provider used the correct standard code.

Contact us if you and your training provider used the correct framework code.

Data lock 07

You and your training provider used a different cost of training and assessment.

What happens next

Check with your training provider to find out which cost is wrong. If the ILR cost is wrong your training provider needs to change it.

You can change the cost in your apprenticeship service account if it’s wrong. You’ll also need to add any costs that change over the apprenticeship.

Contact us if both costs are correct.

Data lock 08

Contact us to fix this issue. You’ll need the unique learner numbers (ULNs) and UK provider reference numbers (UKPRNs) for all apprentices with a data lock 08.

Data lock 09

The start date for the cost of training and assessment is before the start date in your apprenticeship service account.

What happens next

Check with your training provider to find out which start date is wrong.

If the ILR start date is wrong your training provider needs to change it. If the commitment start date is wrong and your training provider has:

  • not approved it, you can change it in your apprenticeship service account
  • approved it, you must stop the commitment and start a new one with the correct date

Contact us if both start dates are correct.
