Transfer apprenticeship funds

Levy-paying employers can transfer up to 25% of their annual apprenticeship funds to other employers. This can help other businesses or charities pay for their apprenticeships.

Who can receive funds

You can transfer funds to any levy or non-levy paying employer, including apprenticeship training agencies (ATA). You cannot receive a transfer if you’ve already sent one in the same tax year.

You can view your transfer allowance in your apprenticeship service account. The amount is calculated using your total declared levy from the previous tax year, with the English percentage and government top-up.

How to transfer your funds

If you don’t spend your apprenticeship funds within 2 years, you lose them.

When you’ve chosen an employer to transfer your funds to, you need to connect your apprenticeship service accounts. You must send the connection invite.

You'll need to agree the cost, apprenticeship standard and number of apprentices with the employer. Once you have signed an ESFA Apprenticeship Employer Agreement, you can transfer the funds from your apprenticeship account.

Connect your accounts

The receiving employer needs to share their account ID with the sending employer. You can find your ID under the company name on your apprenticeship service account homepage.

The sending employer needs to enter the ID into the new transfers section of their account. The account will then display the name and details of the employer you are connecting with.

After you connect accounts, the receiving employer or their training provider can add apprentices to the service. We will send information about the cost, standard and number of apprentices to the sending employer for final approval. We will not display any personal details.

Make a transfer

After the training provider submits their apprenticeship data, payments are made automatically every month. You can view the transactions in the finance section of your account.

The sending employer cannot stop transferring the funds before the apprenticeship ends. Funds will continue to transfer unless the:

  • apprenticeship finishes
  • receiving employer stops the transfer

Transferred funds will also be the first funds to come out of a sending employer’s account each month. So it is important that the account has enough funds to support it and any other financial commitments.

When the transfer is complete the receiving employer is completely responsible for the apprenticeship.
