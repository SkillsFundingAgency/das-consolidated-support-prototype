Managing cohorts and apprentices

When you add apprentices to the employer's apprenticeship service account they’ll go into to a new or existing cohort. The employer needs to approve this cohort after they check:

  • it has the correct apprentices
  • the details are what you both agreed
  • the training and assessment costs are correct
  • that the employer can pay the difference from their own budget if it costs more than the funding band maximum

Change your apprentice's details

You can make changes before the apprenticeship starts or before the employer approves the cohort. After the apprenticeship starts, you can only change the:

  • apprentice's name and date of birth
  • training costs in the first month

If the apprentice wants to change their apprenticeship, you’ll need to stop the current apprenticeship and start a new one.

The employer must approve any changes you make.
