Set training provider permissions

You can give your training provider permission to create cohorts and add apprentices to your apprenticeship service account. You’ll need to approve any information they give to us.

You must add your training provider to your account using their UK provider reference number (UKPRN). You’ll be able to control what tasks they can do on your behalf. You can set, remove or change permissions at any time.

The training provider will not be able to view any other information in your apprenticeship service account.

Create an apprenticeship service account

You can only reserve funding for one apprentice at a time. You’ll need the apprentice’s start date and training course details.

Making changes

Your training provider can invite you to set up an apprenticeship service account. You’ll still need to set their permissions.
