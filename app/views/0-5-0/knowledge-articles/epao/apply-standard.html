Apply to assess a standard

All apprentices doing apprenticeship standards must complete an independent end-point assessment. Only organisations on the register of end-point assessment organisations (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 63KB) can assess apprenticeship standards.

Apply for standards or view the status of your application in your apprenticeship assessment service account.

Join the register

You can apply to join the register. You’ll need to create an account for your organisation before you apply.

The application is to check that your organisation can assess standards.

Once your organisation has joined the register you can:

  • apply to assess standards
  • view your upcoming assessments
  • record assessments
  • apply for certificates
  • manage user permissions

Application processing time

We aim to let you know if your application was successful within 2 weeks, but it can take longer.

Change a standard

To change the standard you’re applying for, cancel your application and apply for a new standard.
