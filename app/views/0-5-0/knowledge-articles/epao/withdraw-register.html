Withdraw your organisation

Contact us to withdraw from a standard or from the register of end-point assessment organisations.

You can call us on 08000 150 600 or email helpdesk@manage.apprenticeships.service.gov.uk. Our opening times are 8am to 8pm, Monday to Friday.

What you'll need

You'll need to know:

  • your organisation’s contact details - this includes the end-point assessment organisation number
  • the type of change (amendment or withdrawal)
  • the date you want to withdraw from the register

Your organisation must also carry out an impact assessment before amending or withdrawing from the register. This includes details of:

  • how your organisation promoted itself to providers or employers and how you’ll let them know you’re withdrawing
  • any employers that selected your organisation and the contracts or payments
  • any end-point assessments your organisation has delivered
