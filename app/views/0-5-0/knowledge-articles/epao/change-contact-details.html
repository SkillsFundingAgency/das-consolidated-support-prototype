Change your organisation's contact details or name

You can change all of your organisation’s contact details in the apprenticeship assessment service account, apart from the organisation name.

If you need to change your organisation’s name, you must update Companies House and then contact us. We will update the register of end-point assessment organisations with your new organisation name.

You can call us on 08000 150 600 or email helpdesk@manage.apprenticeships.service.gov.uk. Our opening times are 8am to 8pm, Monday to Friday.
