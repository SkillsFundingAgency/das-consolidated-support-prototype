Our only apprenticeship service user has left the company - how do we access our account?

Updated 9 November 2017.

You will need to ring us and we will add a new user to your account. You will need:

  • your account ID number
  • the full PAYE scheme of a PAYE that is in the account, and the last YTD submission value; we use this as a security check
  • the name and email address of user that left the company
  • the name and email address of two new users that you wish to add to your account; we recommend that you add two users to avoid this issue reoccurring
